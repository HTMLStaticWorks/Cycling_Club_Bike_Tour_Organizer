Core Performance Modules

Our programs combine in-saddle rides with theory modules to target performance holistically.

Module 01: Fueling & Nutrition

Learn carbohydrate replenishment rates, electrolyte balance protocols, hydration requirements during long, high-elevation climbs, and post-ride protein recovery window principles.

Module 02: Draft Line Mechanics

Understand wind tunnel drafting dynamics, rotating pace-lines (single vs double), safe positioning margins, front-rider duties, and emergency braking communication.

Module 03: Data-Metric Analysis

Introduction to FTP testing, normalized power, heart rate variability, training stress score (TSS) tracking, and custom target zones for training optimization.
