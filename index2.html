FAQ & Safety Guidance

Answers to gravel routes, support mechanics, and physical prep.

We highly suggest tires width between 38mm and 45mm, set up tubeless at lower pressures, to navigate loose rock fragments safely on gravel traverses.

Every VeloMax tour rider receives a pre-packed saddle bag containing spare tubes, tyre levers, multi-tool, and Co2 inflators. Our accompanying tour marshals carry extensive replacement parts and alignment tools.
