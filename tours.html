Fitness & Difficulty Legend

Beginner
Flat / Multi-use path
Suited for anyone who can ride a bicycle. Minimal elevation (< 150m), comfortable pace, regular breaks.
Intermediate
Rolling hills / Gravel paths
Requires moderate aerobic stamina. Up to 80km distance, some climbing (300m-600m), moderate paces.
Advanced
Steep climbs / Technical trails
Designed for active cyclists. Elevation metrics up to 1,200m, paces exceeding 25km/h on road segments.
Expert / Elite
High alpine passes / Rough singletrack
Requires peak fitness. Long days in saddle (120km+), substantial climbs (> 1,500m), fast-paced group drafts.
