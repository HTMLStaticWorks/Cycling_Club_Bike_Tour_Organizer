Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a cycling tour?

Visit our Tours page, select your category, and click "Book Tour". You can also submit an inquiry via this contact form.

Can I rent a bike without joining a tour?

Yes! We offer daily bike rentals from our HQ. Select "Bike Fleet Rentals" in the inquiry form or call the support desk directly.

What is the refund policy for tours?

Full refunds are available up to 14 days before the tour departure. Within 7 days, a 50% refund applies. Contact us for special circumstances.

Do you offer corporate event packages?

Absolutely. We design custom corporate cycling days, team retreats, and brand activations. Select "Corporate Events" in the inquiry form above.
