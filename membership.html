VeloMax Ride Safety Code

  1. Helmets are Mandatory: Absolutely no rider will join any VeloMax group spin or tour without a certified cycling helmet.
  2. Respect Group Leaders: Always stay behind the lead marshal. Do not pass or split the draft lane unless directed by a leader.
  3. Signal Hazards: Point and call out gravel patch, potholes, glass, or merging cars to protect the cyclists behind you.
  4. No Headsets or Earbuds: Riders must remain alert to calls and merging traffic. Bone-conduction setups are allowed at low volume.
  5. Traffic Compliance: Stop at red lights and stop signs. We cycle as vehicles and respect local community road laws.
